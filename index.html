Who We Are

Girls Who Code is a national nonprofit organization dedicated to bridging the gender gap in computing fields. Girls Who Code clubs around the country aim to teach girls programming basics in JavaScript, HTML, and Python.

What We Do

We want to create a club at Menlo where girls can learn computing skills and languages not taught in school programming courses, and collaborate on coding projects in a relaxed and supportive environment. We want to give our peers the tools to design and build creative, useful apps and websites. We also hope to plan field trips to tech companies nearby and invite engineers, designers, and managers to come speak to us.
